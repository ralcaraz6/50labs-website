07 — FAQ

Questions, answered straight

Software that does a job, not just answers a prompt. An agent reads context, makes decisions within rules you set, and takes action across your tools — replying to a lead, booking a meeting, updating your CRM. Think of it as a digital teammate with a narrow, well-defined role.

Most agents go live in under 30 days. Week one is discovery and data access, weeks two and three are build and testing against real historical conversations, then we deploy with guardrails on. Complex multi-agent setups can take 6 to 8 weeks.

Nothing technical. The stack is ours end to end — we build it, host it, watch it and improve it. From you we need the person who knows the process best: two or three hours during setup, then a short review once a month.

Every agent ships with confidence thresholds and escalation rules: anything ambiguous, sensitive or high-value goes to a human with the full conversation attached. We also review transcripts weekly during the first month and tune the agent before edge cases become patterns.

Everything is processed and stored inside the EU with end-to-end encryption. We sign a DPA with every client, collect only what each workflow strictly needs, and your data never feeds anyone else's models. Dedicated EU model hosting is available on request.

Anything with an API — and that covers almost everything: WhatsApp, Gmail, Outlook, Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, Stripe, Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets and hundreds more. If a tool has no API, we can usually bridge it through Make or Zapier.

Launch is the start, not the end. Your plan includes monitoring, error handling, model updates and continuous tuning based on real conversations. You get a monthly report with hard numbers and a call to decide what the agent should learn next.
